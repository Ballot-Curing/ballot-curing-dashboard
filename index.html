Welcome to the U.S. Ballot Curing Project

A platform for resources related to ballot curing - bringing efficiency and transparency to our democracy

View ballot status analytics for a given election. Charts and figures illustrate statistics on rejected and cured ballots, broken down by location and demographics.
Download a list of voters whose ballots have been rejected in a selected election. Lists can be filtered by parameters such as location, voter demographics, and ballot issue. The data is sourced from the selected state's Secretary of State Office.
